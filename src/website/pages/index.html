The Ogopogo Solar Vision

Ogopogo Solar is a student-run solar car team at the University of British Columbia. We are a group of students from various disciplines who are passionate about sustainability and renewable energy. Our goal is to design and build a solar-powered car to compete in the American Solar Challenge and the Formula Sun Grand Prix. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable transportation and inspiring the next generation of engineers and innovators.
